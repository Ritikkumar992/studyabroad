Welcome To Armenia.

Armenia is a country, and previous Soviet republic, in the rocky Caucasus area amongst Asia and Europe. Among the soonest Christian civic establishments, it’s characterized by religious destinations including the Greco-Roman Temple of Garni and fourth-century Etchmiadzin Cathedral, the central station of the Armenian Church.

  • Number of Universities: 45 (MCI approved: 6).
  • Average cost of stay (Fee included): 25-45 lakhs.
  • Affordable and Advanced Learning.
  • Course Duration: 6 years.
  • A warm and welcoming culture.

The climate is highland continental, dry with four seasons. Temperatures in Armenia can vary considerably between seasons. The summer is generally pleasant reaching 25 degree Celsius, though in the Ararat valley temperatures can climb to 40 degree Celsius.
